My Favorite Strategy: RSI + EMA

This strategy combines the power of the Relative Strength Index (RSI) and Exponential Moving Average (EMA).

  • Use 14-period RSI
  • Use 50 and 200 EMA
  • Buy Signal: RSI < 30 and price crosses above EMA50
  • Sell Signal: RSI > 70 and price crosses below EMA50

Timeframe: 1-minute to 15-minute (scalping)
